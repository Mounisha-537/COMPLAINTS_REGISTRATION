# User Flow & Interaction Guidelines

This document details the navigation steps and workflows for the three user roles: Customers (Ordinary Users), Agents (Officers/Technicians), and Admins.

---

## 👤 1. Customer (Ordinary User) Flow

### Role Description:
Create and manage complaints, interact with agents, and manage profile information.

### Operational Steps:
1. **Registration and Login**:
   - Create an account by providing necessary information, including name, email, phone number, and password.
   - Log in securely using registered credentials.
2. **Complaint Submission**:
   - Fill out the complaint form with details of the issue (including description, address, city, state, pincode, and relevant attachments).
   - Submit the complaint for backend validation and processing.
3. **Status Tracking**:
   - View the status of all submitted complaints in the dashboard or status list page.
   - Monitor real-time status updates as the complaint transitions through states.
4. **Interaction with Agents**:
   - Connect with assigned agents/officers directly using the built-in messaging chat console.
   - Discuss complaints further and provide additional details or clarification.
5. **Profile Management**:
   - Manage personal profile information, including contact details and address registers.

---

## 🛠️ 2. Agent (Officer / Technician) Flow

### Role Description:
Manage complaints assigned by the admin, communicate with customers, and update complaint statuses.

### Operational Steps:
1. **Registration and Login**:
   - Create an officer account using email and password.
   - Log in using registered credentials (access is pending Admin authorization).
2. **Complaint Management**:
   - Access the agent dashboard to view, search, and manage complaints assigned by the administrator.
   - Review complaint details, addresses, and attachment files.
3. **Customer Interaction**:
   - Respond to citizen inquiries, answer questions, and address feedback directly.
   - Communicate with customers regarding their complaints through the built-in chat window.
4. **Status Update**:
   - Change the status of complaints based on resolution or progress stages (`In Progress`, `Resolved`, `Rejected`).
   - Provide real-time updates and comments to customers regarding the status of their complaints.

---

## 🔑 3. Admin Flow

### Role Description:
Oversee the overall operation of the complaint registration platform, manage complaints, users, and agents, and enforce platform policies.

### Operational Steps:
1. **Management and Monitoring**:
   - Monitor and moderate all incoming complaints submitted by users to filter out spam or duplicate issues.
   - Audit system activity to ensure SLA timelines are met.
2. **Complaint Assignment**:
   - Assign complaints to the desired agents/officers based on workload, region, and expertise.
   - Ensure timely, fair, and efficient routing of complaints.
3. **User and Agent Management**:
   - Manage user and agent accounts, including registration, login authorizations, and profile information.
   - Verify and approve officer accounts and enforce platform security policies, terms of service, and privacy regulations.
4. **Continuous Improvement**:
   - Implement operational measures to improve the platform's functionality, user experience, and security controls.
   - Review and address any issues or concerns raised by users or agents for better service delivery.
